Inheritance

The easiest way to describe inheritance is through the classic example, geometric shapes. There are really two types of shapes: ellipses (which are rounded) and polygons (which have certain number of sides) Circle is type of ellipse with one focus; triangles, rectangles and pentagons are types of polygons with different number of sides. A square is a type of rectangle with all sides equal. This describes the perfect inheritance relationship.
