Validating Master Credit Cards

If you own or operate an e-commerce site, chances are you need to deal with credit card validation. Not every incorrect credit card number is a fraudulent buyer; sometimes people just mistype or hit Enter too early. To prevent a trip back to the server, you can create some basic patterns to determine if a given credit card number is valid.

To start, consider a MasterCard credit card number, which must contain 16 digits. Of those 16 digits, the first two digits must be a number between 51 and 55.
