Non-Capturing Groups

Groups that create backreferences are called capturing groups. There are also non-capturing groups, which don't create backreferences. In very long regular expressions, storing backreferences slows down the matching process. By using non-capturing groups, you can have the same flexibility to match sequences of characters without incurring the overhead of storing the results.
