Computed styles in IE

In addition to the style object of elements and CSS rules is the computed style of an element. The computed style is made up of all the style information from inline styles and CSS rules to give a true indication of how the element is being represented on the screen. As usual, Internet Explorer and the DOM differ in their implementations.
