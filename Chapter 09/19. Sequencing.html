Sequencing

It takes a mousedown event followed by a mouseup event on the same target before a click event fires. Likewise, to get a dblclick event to fire, it takes the following sequence of events on the same target:

  1. mousedown
  2. mouseup
  3. click
  4. mousedown
  5. mouseup
  6. click
  7. dblclick

Note: 6 events will be triggered before a double click event is called.
